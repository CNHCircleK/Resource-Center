Communications & Marketing

The Communications & Marketing committee is composed of members responsible for bolstering the public image of CNH Circle K! From providing resources through manuals and guides to releasing CNH content such as the Sunburst, SunnyTV, and the Sunspot, the C&M committee is working hard to help define the brand that we all associate with CNH!
