District Files

Find District Board Files, Applications, the ERF, and more in this collection of CNH Circle K files!

Communications & Marketing

The Communications & Marketing committee is composed of members responsible for bolstering the public image of CNH Circle K! From providing resources through manuals and guides to releasing CNH content such as the Sunburst, SunnyTV, and the Sunspot, the C&M committee is working hard to help define the brand that we all associate with CNH!

Kiwanis Family

The Kiwanis Family and Foundation Committee is responsible for everything Kiwanis Family Related! We're here to help you learn more about the Kiwanis Family, build your Kiwanis Family Relations by connecting you with the different Kiwanis Family Branches, and further strengthen them by providing more ideas and opportunities that you can enact on.

Member Recognition

The Member Recognition Committee is responsible for the distribution of District awards, thinking of new outlets for recognition, and the Membership Recognition Program. Here you will be able to find updated and improved District awards, manuals for said awards and recognition, the Master Records Sheet, and more!

Secretary

Secretaries are responsible for keeping accurate club records and being one of the primary liaisons between their clubs and the District. Here, you will find resources regarding various forms and documents!

Service

The Service Committee is dedicated to the upholding the service tenet of Circle K International by planning the District Large Scale Service Project (DLSSP) yearly and by making service officers more productive by providing resources that make their tasks easier.

Technology

The Technology Committee is devoted to providing the clubs of CNH with resources to aid in the creation of club websites, hosting webinars, and anything else tech-related. Here you will find examples of website features done well, and much more to come!

Treasurer/Fundraising

The Finance and Fundraising Committee work to support Treasurers and Fundraising Chairs of the District in their positions to ensure that they feel confident and capable during their terms. Here you will be able to find resources for on managing a budget, event planning, and implementing successful fundraising efforts!

Workshop Slides

From Fall Training Conference to District Convention to Spring Training Conference, our Circle K members host workshops on several occasions throughout the year. Check out their workshop slides for a taste of what was presented, and appreciate the time and effort they've have put in to make them available!
