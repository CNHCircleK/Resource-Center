Kiwanis Family

The Kiwanis Family and Foundation Committee is responsible for everything Kiwanis Family Related! We're here to help you learn more about the Kiwanis Family, build your Kiwanis Family Relations by connecting you with the different Kiwanis Family Branches, and further strengthen them by providing more ideas and opportunities that you can enact on. If you need someone to guide you, please do not be afraid to contact the chair or anyone on the committee!

Member Recognition

The Member Recognition Committee is responsible for the distribution of District awards, thinking of new outlets for recognition, and the Membership Recognition Program. Here you will be able to find updated and improved District awards, manuals for said awards and recognition, the Master Records Sheet, and more!
